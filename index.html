ABOUT US

UPES aws set up in 2003 through the UPES Act,2003 of the State Legialature of Uttrakhand. The University is approved by UGC and accredited by NBA.It had an aspiring dream and a distinctive methodology, which allowed the University to develop itself a a novel foundation providing industry-engaged focussed UG and PG courses, some of which caters to high- growth regions like cloud computing, mechanical engineering, civil engineering, aerospace engineering, design engineering, public administration, etc.

UPES Dehradun was the head institute to present industry internship ti o its faculty, it has educational alliances with 40 global partners in 18 countries.
